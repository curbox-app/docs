Curbox is completely free, open source, and available directly from our GitHub page. Because it is simple and open, there is absolutely no need to log into the Play Store.


## What Your Device Needs

Before starting, just make sure you have an Android device running Oreo or anything newer, and about eight megabytes of free space scattered somewhere on your phone.

We also highly recommend grabbing an app called Shizuku if you want absolutely rock solid blocking power, though it is fully optional!


## A Quick Note on Safety Warnings

When you start installing Curbox, your phone might suddenly throw a scary warning at you, usually from a system called Google Play Protect. Do not let this alarm you! This happens no matter which installation method you choose.

To understand why this happens, think about what Curbox actually does. To successfully restrict your screen time, Curbox requires very deep and highly sensitive system permissions. To an automated robot security guard like Play Protect, an app asking to take control over your other apps looks highly suspicious. But for a focus tool, taking control is the entire point! 

You can rest completely assured that Curbox is totally safe because it is "open source." What does open source mean? Imagine a restaurant with glass walls where you can clearly watch the chef cook. Anyone can walk by and see exactly what ingredients are being gathered. In the same way, every single line of code that makes up Curbox is completely public on the internet. Thousands of tech experts around the world can look inside and verify that there is absolutely nothing malicious hiding in the recipe.

But please do not worry, this absolutely does not mean that your personal data is out in the open! While the ingredients used to build the app are public for everyone to see, your private information stays securely locked on your very own phone. Your daily habits, the apps you choose to block, and your schedules never leave your device. We built a glass kitchen so you can trust the chefs, but your personal life remains entirely your own secret.

So, if Google suddenly tries to block the installation, simply tap "More details" and confidently choose "Install anyway." If it is acting extra stubborn, you can even open your Google Play Store app, tap your profile picture, and temporarily turn off Play Protect just long enough to welcome Curbox onto your device.


## The Installation Steps

### Direct Download

<Steps>
1. **Download the File**
   Visit our GitHub page and happily download the newest file that simply says "apk".

2. **Allow The File**
   On your Android phone, head into Settings, then Apps, then Special App Access. Find the section named "Install Unknown Apps." Find your browser or file app and flip the switch to allow the file. This just means you trust apps from outside the Play Store!

3. **Install the App**
   Open the file you just downloaded. Tap Install. If you see a warning from Play Protect, remember our safety note above and confidently install anyway! Take a quick breath while it finishes, and you are almost done.

4. **Unlock Restricted Settings**
   Because you downloaded a raw apk file outside of a store, newer Android phones add one final safety lock. This is totally normal for powerful apps! 
   
   To fix this, go to your phone Settings, tap on Apps, and select Curbox. Look for the three tiny dots sitting in the top right corner of your screen. Tap them, and select "Allow restricted settings." This unlocks the door for Curbox to start protecting your time!

5. **Open the Magic**
   Find Curbox hiding in your app drawer and simply tap to launch your journey.
</Steps>


### The Easy Way: Using an App Store

We are incredibly proud to be available on F Droid! This is a wonderful, trusted app store specifically built to hold free and open source Android apps. F Droid double checks every single build to ensure maximum safety for your phone.

<Steps>
1. First, search for and install the F Droid companion app on your phone if you do not have it yet.
2. Next, launch the F Droid store and type "Curbox" into the search bar.
3. Then simply tap Install! F Droid takes care of everything else completely automatically.
</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
Sometimes our store releases fall a few days behind our website releases, just because the wonderful folks at F Droid need time to thoroughly check the new build for complete safety.
</Aside>
