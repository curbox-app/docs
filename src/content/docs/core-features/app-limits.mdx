## How the App Blocker Works

Curbox is your friendly companion for staying focused. It keeps an eye on the apps you are using and gently blocks the ones that distract you, based entirely on the rules you create.

Here is a simple guide to using the app blocker:

<Steps>
1. **Create a New Group**
   Open Curbox and head over to the "Reducers" screen. You can think of this as your command center. Select "App Pause" and tap the Add button in the bottom right corner to start a new group of blocked apps.

2. **Pick Your Apps**
   You will see a big list of the apps installed on your phone. Simply tap the ones you find distracting. All the rules you set next will apply to the apps you choose here.

3. **Decide How to Block**
   Curbox gives you two easy ways to limit your app time.
   
   First, you can block by usage. This means you tell Curbox, "Please block Instagram if I use it for more than an hour today." 
   
   Second, you can block by time of day. This is perfect if you only want to allow certain apps at specific times, like letting yourself check Facebook only between 7 PM and 9 PM on weekends.

4. **Set Your Limits**
   Tap the "Configure Settings" button. Now, just dial in the daily limits or the exact time ranges you decided on. It is really that easy!
</Steps>

## The Warning Screen

When you try to open a blocked app outside your allowed time or after you spent your limit, Curbox steps in with a warning screen. This friendly reminder gives you a moment to pause and decide if you really need to open the app right now.

You can even write a custom message to yourself! Something like "Remember your goals" will show up on this screen.

## Unlocking Options

We know that sometimes you truly need to use an app. The warning screen gives you choices on how to proceed. 

**If you want strict limits:**
You can set things up so there is no way around the block. You can either be sent right back to your home screen immediately, or see your warning message but still be locked out.

**If you just need a little flexibility:**
Curbox lets you earn more time. You can pick an option that lets you choose exactly how many extra minutes you need. Or, you can set a fixed amount of bonus time.

For an extra challenge, you can require yourself to scan a specific barcode or QR code around your house to get more time. You could also make yourself type out a full sentence, like "I promise I only need five minutes." Another great option is the intent log, where you have to type out why you are opening the app. Your reason will stay on screen as a gentle reminder while you use the app.

If you often unlock apps by habit, you can turn on a required waiting period. This forces you to stop and wait a few moments before the app unlocks. You can even enable maximum brightness to wake your brain up and help you make a thoughtful choice.
