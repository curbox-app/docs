The **Usage** tab shows you exactly where your time goes. You get a weekly chart, a breakdown by app, and website stats for every browser you use.

## Reading Your Stats

The top of the screen shows **TOTAL TODAY** — your total screen time so far today. Below it is a bar chart covering the current week. Tap any day's bar to see that day's total.

Use the arrows to browse previous weeks.

Scroll down to see a list of every app you used, with today's time shown next to each one.


## Website Stats

If you use a supported browser, Curbox tracks which websites you visited and for how long. The website stats appear below the app list, grouped by browser.

Supported browsers:

- Google Chrome
- Brave
- Firefox
- Firefox F-Droid
- Opera
- Cromite
- Vanadium (GrapheneOS)


## Ignoring Apps

Some apps run in the background and inflate your stats. To hide an app from the list, add it to the ignore list. Tap the three-dot menu at the top right and choose **Ignore List**, then add the apps you want to exclude.


## Exporting Your Data

You can export your usage data as a CSV file to open in a spreadsheet. Tap the three-dot menu and choose **Export**. Three options are available:

- **Daily Breakdown** — one row per app per day
- **Total Summary** — one total row per app
- **Both** — a file that includes both views


## Home Screen Widgets

Curbox offers two home screen widgets so you can see your stats without opening the app.

- **Screentime Widget** — shows your total screen time today
- **Reels Widget** — shows how many short form videos you have watched today

To add a widget, tap the three-dot menu and choose **Pin Widget**, then select the one you want.

<Aside type="tip">
Placing the Screentime Widget on your home screen is one of the simplest things you can do. Awareness of your usage — without any blocking — is often enough to nudge you toward better habits.
</Aside>
