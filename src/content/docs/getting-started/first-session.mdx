Now that you have Curbox installed, let us get you started with your very first focused session. It is fun and quick, and you will be totally focused in less than one minute.


## What Is a Focused Session?

A focused session is simply a chunk of time you set aside completely for deep work, study, or just relaxing without your phone bothering you. When a session starts, Curbox automatically blocks all those apps you choose to put aside. If you accidentally tap a blocked app, Curbox instantly closes it to snap you right back on track.

You can set these sessions to start all by themselves every single day, or you can turn them on manually whenever you need the power to focus.

You get two easy ways to focus.
First is Auto Focus. With this, you pick exactly when a session should start and how long it should run automatically.
Second is Manual Focus. With this, you just start a session on the spot for however long you need it right then and there.


## Let Us Start Together

<Steps>
1. **Open The Focus Screen**
   Launch Curbox and gently tap the big Focus button at the bottom of the screen.

2. **Pick Your Time**
   You can drag the slider to choose any amount of time you want. You can also tap the quick buttons for common times like fifteen or thirty minutes. For this very first try, we suggest just picking two or three minutes so you can see how nice it feels.

   <Aside type="tip">
   Studies say that ninety minutes is the perfect limit for deep work. If you are just starting your journey to focus, a twenty five minute session is a wonderful starting point for your brain.
   </Aside>

3. **About Focus Groups**
   Curbox uses the concept of groups to organize your apps. This lets you make a setup once, and then reuse it over and over again without thinking. Let us create your first group now by tapping "Create New Group."

4. **Making Your First Group**
   First, give it a friendly name that you will recognize. Next, select all the apps you want to put on pause during this session.
   
   Finally, there is an option to either let yourself quit early or not. If you turn this off, there is no going back once you start! Your device will not let you quit. When you feel good about your group, press "Save Group."

5. **Start Your Session**
   Now that you are back on the focus screen, choose your brand new group and tap "Start Focus." Curbox steps aside and quietly minimizes itself. You will see a friendly little notification letting you know exactly how much time you have left.
</Steps>
