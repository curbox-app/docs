After you install Curbox, a friendly setup wizard will help you give the app the permissions it needs. This page explains what each permission does and why you need it, in simple terms.


## Granting Permissions

Curbox needs a few permissions to work its magic. The setup wizard will show you buttons to grant each permission step by step. Just follow the simple instructions on your screen.

<Aside type="tip">
If you already use an app called Shizuku, you can skip the setup hassle and give all permissions at once with a single click!
</Aside>

If you installed Curbox as an APK file from our website or GitHub, your phone might hide some settings at first. Do not worry! You just need to enable restricted settings. 

To do this, go into your Settings, then down to All Apps, and find Curbox. Tap the three little dots at the top right, and choose "Enable restricted settings." That is it!

If you install Curbox using the F Droid app store, this step is handled completely automatically for you.


## Your Next Steps

Congratulations, your device is now ready! You can head over to "Your First Focused Session" to create your very first session.
