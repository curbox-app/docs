Curbox is an Android app that helps you spend less time on your phone. It gives you tools to set limits, block distractions, and build healthier habits.


## What Curbox Can Do

Curbox has three main areas, each with a different job.

**Usage** shows you how much time you spend in each app, which websites you visit most, and how your habits change week by week.

**Focus** lets you start a timed session where Curbox blocks whatever you choose, so you can get things done without interruptions.

**Reducers** is where you set up all your limits and nudges. You can pause specific apps, block short form videos, hide distracting parts of websites, and more.

## How Curbox Works

Curbox runs quietly in the background using Android's Accessibility Service. When you open an app that you have limited, Curbox steps in and shows you a pause screen. You choose what happens next — whether you can proceed, how long for, and how much effort it takes.

No data leaves your device. Everything is stored locally.
