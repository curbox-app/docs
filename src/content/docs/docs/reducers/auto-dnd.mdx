**Auto DND** silences your notifications the moment you open a chosen app. When you leave the app, Do Not Disturb turns off automatically. It helps you stay present and cuts down the urge to bounce between apps while you are already on your phone.


## Setting It Up

![The Auto DND screen showing an empty state with No Auto DND Groups yet message and a + button](../../assets/auto-dnd-screen.png)
*Tap the + button to add your first group.*

<Steps>
1. **Open Auto DND**
   Tap **Reducers**, then tap **Auto DND**.

2. **Tap the + button**
   The **+** button is in the bottom right corner.

3. **Name your group**
   Type a name for this group, like "Social Apps" or "Evening Browsing."

4. **Select apps**
   Choose the apps that should trigger Do Not Disturb when opened.

5. **Save**
   Tap **Done**. Auto DND is now active for those apps.
</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
Auto DND requires the Do Not Disturb permission. If you skipped it during setup, Curbox will ask you to grant it when you try to enable this feature. Tap the prompt and follow the on-screen instructions.
</Aside>
